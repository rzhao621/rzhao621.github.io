About Me

I graduated from Yale University with a B.S. in Earth and Planetary Sciences (concentration: energy and environment). I also received an Energy Studies Certificate, equivalent to a minor, which helped me develop the knowledge and skills needed for energy-related fields. My interests pertain to renewable energy, clean transportation, remote sensing, GIS, sustainability, and environmental data science. Currently, I am a data analyst at Ivy Energy. We are a software startup transforming the multifamily housing landscape by enabling profitable solar and making clean energy more accessible.

In my free time, you can find me experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, exploring the San Diego food scene, or enjoying my time in nature.
